Commercial Use of the ISS

The International Space Station is not only a scientific laboratory but also a platform for private industry, tourism, and new business opportunities in space. Companies use the ISS to test technologies, develop new products, and even send paying customers to orbit. This commercial use is helping open low-Earth orbit to more people and paving the way for future private space stations.

Commercial AreaExamples
Cargo & TransportSpaceX Dragon, Northrop Grumman Cygnus, Boeing Starliner
Space TourismPrivate astronauts flying with SpaceX Crew Dragon and Soyuz
Private ResearchPharmaceuticals, materials science, in-orbit manufacturing
Media & OutreachLive broadcasts, education programs, filming (e.g. space movies)
New TechnologyTesting satellites, robotics, and life-support systems for business use
Future ExpansionCommercial modules (Axiom Space) preparing for independent stations
