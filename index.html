What is ISS Modelling?

The International Space Station (ISS) is one of, if not, the greatest achievements of human engineering and international cooperation. Our student-led project at Haileybury recreates the ISS using 3d printing at a scale of 1 : 100, allowing students to explore its structure and learn about its design, assembly, and purpose.
