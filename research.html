Research and Science on the ISS

The International Space Station is not only a home in orbit but also the world’s premier microgravity laboratory. Astronauts and scientists use its unique environment to study fundamental science, test new technologies, and conduct experiments that would be impossible on Earth. The results improve life at home while preparing humanity for future exploration of the Moon, Mars, and beyond.
